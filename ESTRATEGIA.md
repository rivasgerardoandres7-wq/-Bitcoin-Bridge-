# 🌉 Bitcoin Bridge: Project Strategy

> **Date:** April 1, 2026  
> **Status:** Data Collection Phase (Year-over-Year)

## 📋 Project Vision
Bitcoin Bridge is a tool designed for local businesses in El Salvador. Our goal is to track and compare retail prices from 2025 to 2026, showing how Bitcoin adoption and market evolution affect daily commerce on a yearly basis.
